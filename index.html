About X-Engi

Founded in 2024, X-Engi has grown from a small machine shop to a leading supplier of precision castings and machined components. Our commitment to quality, precision, and customer service has earned us long-term partnerships with industry leaders.

With skilled technicians and engineers, we combine traditional craftsmanship with cutting-edge technology to deliver components that meet the most demanding specifications.
